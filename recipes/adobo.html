ADOBO

Pork Adobo

It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. There are version wherein onions are also added. Adobo is a popular dish in the Philippines, along with Sinigang. Adobo, in general, can be cooked using different kinds of protein.

Ingredients

  • pork
  • vinegar
  • soy sauce
  • garlic
  • onions
  • sugar
  • bayleaf
  • cooking oil
  • pepper corns

Steps

  1. Fry onions, garlic, then pork till brown
  2. add half a cup of soy sauce
  3. add water till it covers pork then boil add pepper corns and bayleaves
  4. after 30 min let simmer then add 1/3 cup of vinegar
  5. simmer for 15 min, remove lid then wait till sauce reduces
  6. add sugar to taste
  7. done and dusted, out of this world
